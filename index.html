SNAKE

Rules to play!

How to play: Use arrows [↑→↓←] keys to move snake in any direction where you want. The snake can't take back move from same coming direction. You can select the difficulty level according to you'r choice. Your current score and high score is shown at the right side. After enjoying the game you can also give us feedback. Enjoy the wonderful and challenging Game of The Real Snake, world's first game ever!

TIC TAC TOE

Rules to play!

How to play: Use Mouse to mark any grid of your choice. This is two players game, first player will mark the grid as 'X' and second player will mark the grid as 'O'. The one who completes a vertical, horizontal or diagonal line of their respective mark will win the game. After enjoying the game you can also give us feedback. Enjoy the wonderful and challenging Game of Tic Tac Toe, play with brain.

Dive Ball

Rules to play!

How to play: Use Arrow keys [→ ←] to move the ball. This is a single player game. The ball will be destroyed if it touches at the top. Passing one bar will add one point to your score. After enjoying the game you can also give us feedback. Enjoy the wonderful and challenging Game of Dive Ball, Stay away from the Top.
