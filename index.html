MARIO

Rules to play?

How to play Use arrows [↑→↓←] or W-A-S-D keys to move Mario, to jump higher hold the button. Use Shift/CTRL to Fire/Sprint. Use P to pause and M to mute. You can play continuously or select a level out of 32 any time you want. There is also an option to generate a random map. It's an infinite fun! Enjoy the wonderful and challenging world of Mario, the most famous plumber guy ever!
